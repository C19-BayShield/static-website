Help Protect Those On The Frontlines

Professional makers, coders, and designers all around the Bay Area have joined forces to facilitate the Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) shortage in medical facilities happening locally and around the globe due to the COVID-19 pandemic.


Donate

Let's Fight This

By leveraging the maker community to supply our medical facilities with the critical personal protective equipment they need, C19 | BayShield provides swift emergency resource allocation and distribution. We handle the supply chain of PPE so each individual component does what they do best, devoid of additional stress.

Makers

Leveraging the maker community and their combined capabilities to produce personal protective equipment through a multitude of means such as 3D printing.

Collection Hubs

Centralized locations for intaking the personal protective equipment produced by the makers. Collection hubs are responsible for the final stage of assembly and sanitization.

fingerprint

Hospitals

Who this is all for. The hospitals and medical professionals That need the personal protective equipment submit a request and C19 | BayShield covers processing to delivery.

The Mission

We provide the most up to date information to the maker community for producing PPE and ensure a high level of quality control. C19 | BayShield collects fabricated PPE and delivers to a central collection hub while simultaneously distributing the complete PPE to hospitals in need. Our algorithms prioritize facilities that need it the most and complete full scale supply chain logistics so the PPE arrives in a timely manner!
